What's different about Manage?

Manage provides all the functionality your team needs, without the complexity. Our Software is tailor-made for modern digital product teams

01

Track company-wide progress

See how your day-to-day tasks fit into the wider version. Go from tracking progress at the milestone level all the way done to the smallest of details. Never lose sight of the bigger picture again.

02

Advanced built-in reports

Set internal delivery estimates and track progress towards company goals. Our customisable dashboard helps you build out the reports you need to keep key stakeholders informed.

03

Everything you need in one place

Stop jumping from one service to another to communicate, store files, track tasks and share documents. Manage offers an all-in-one team productivity solution.
